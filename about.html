About NeonBlog

Hi, I'm Sandiso — a designer and writer who believes the web should be both functional and fun. I started NeonBlog to share my obsession with clean code, bold aesthetics, and content that actually helps people.

After years of building sites for clients, I noticed that many blogs either looked great but loaded slowly, or were fast but boring. My mission is to show that you can have both: a lightning‑quick, mobile‑friendly blog with personality and punch.

When I'm not tweaking CSS or writing posts, you'll find me exploring new coffee shops or snapping neon signs for inspiration.

This blog is for you if:

  • You want to start a blog that stands out.
  • You love clean design with a splash of color.
  • You value readability and fast loading times.
