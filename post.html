How to Design with Vibrant Colors

Published June 10, 2026 · 5 min read · by Sandiso

Neon pinks and electric purples can transform a dull blog into an unforgettable experience. But using vibrant colors effectively requires balance. In this post, I'll share my top strategies for incorporating bold hues while maintaining readability and professionalism.

Start with a Neutral Base

White, off‑white, or light gray backgrounds let your vibrant accents shine without causing eye strain. Read our guide on typography for more on contrast.

Use Color for Call‑to‑Actions

Buttons, links, and important headings are perfect candidates for your neon palette. Just be consistent – pick one or two accent colors and stick with them.
